    • WHO ARE WE?

    Petals is made up of a group of indiviaduals who are totally in love with producing flowers, fighting climate change and will love to produce plants and flowers for sale to customers, for beautification, and to aid them share powerful, loving messages to their loved ones. We are locally based, but not in a particular region, as we hope to distribute all round the country. On that note, we hope that you patronize us and that we meet your standards.

    WHAT DO WE DO?

    We unite local florists and give them a medium to sell their products, while producing flowers ourselves. We also provide labour for gardening, carriage and other plant related services. We offer delivery to the homes of our customers. Finally, we sell flower related goods such as vases, designed floor mats, and cookies associated with flowers on various occassions.
